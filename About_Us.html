Welcome to HTML, the newest and hippest lunch destination this side of Lock Haven. Hey! That's My Lunch! was founded in 2016 by two visionaries with the sole purpose to provide the greatest lunch variety in town. Built along the winding back of the Susquehanna River, HTML is the first ever building allowed to be physically built on the levee which protects the fine town of Lock Haven. HTML is the first technology themed lunch destination from State College to Williamsport! The founders of HTML are Lock Haven University's very own Lucas Reid and Jerry Stimer who love lunch and computers so much they merged the two into a happy medium which we all know and love today as Hey! That's My lunch!
